Have a Project?

So you have something that you think would make sense as a Codice Foundation project, or you need help getting your project into a more collaborative environment?  Well, we'd definitely like to discuss it.

It's probably best to start by communicating through the Google group - but always feel free to contact us directly via email at info@codice.org
